Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Lampside ("we," "our," or "us") is a product of The Difference Is HIM, LLC (TDIH). We are committed to protecting your privacy and your family's data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Lampside app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique user identifier and optionally your email address.
  • Story preferences: Hero name, age, Bible adventure selection, and mood — used solely to personalize your child's story.
  • Saved stories: Stories you choose to save are stored securely in your account.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information (story count, subscription status) stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase information: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized Bible bedtime stories for your child
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription
  • To save and retrieve your story library
  • To provide customer support

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

Lampside uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI story generation via our secure proxy server (Privacy Policy)
  • ElevenLabs — voice narration of stories (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, in-app purchases (Privacy Policy)
  • Google — Sign in with Google (Privacy Policy)

Story content (hero name, age, adventure) is sent to our secure proxy server to generate your story. This data is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the request.

4. Children's Privacy

Lampside is designed to be used by parents on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Parents manage all account activity. The child's name used in stories is stored only as part of your saved stories and is never shared or sold.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication is handled by industry-standard providers (Apple, Google, Supabase). We do not store payment information.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of any future communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below or use the Delete Account option in the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Lampside after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
